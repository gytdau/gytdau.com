This is one of my earliest projects that I still have, and one of the first projects I've put onto GitHub.

After struggling to write an English essay because of constantly rephrasing, I made Draftil: it's a word processor but you can't delete words afer you've finished typing them.

(Only later did I realise that Rough Draft, an iOS application, already did the same thing. A learning experience none-the-less!)

Writing about this from the future, I realise that I did a lot of things right and a lot of things wrong. The power of hindsight!
